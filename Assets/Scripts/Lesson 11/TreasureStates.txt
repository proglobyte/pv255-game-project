States:
0. Inactive and empty (not interactive)
1. Closed without key in inventory (interactive)
2. Closed with key in inventory (interactive)
3. Opened (not interactive - handled from item)

Actions if clicked:
1. -> 1. (Treasure is still closed, locked sound)
2. -> 3. (Treasure opens, open sound)

Other objects interaction:
1. -> 2. (Key - Treasure unlockes)
3. -> 0. (Sword - Treasure closes, close sound)
